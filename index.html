FAQ

Common questions

Does PenCast work with Photoshop and Clip Studio? +

Yes. PenCast injects stylus events directly into macOS and Windows — so Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, Figma, and any other drawing app see the S Pen as a real Wacom tablet with full pressure and tilt support.

Which Samsung devices work with PenCast? +

Any Samsung Galaxy with a built-in S Pen — Galaxy S Ultra series, Galaxy Note series, Galaxy Z Fold series, and Galaxy Tab S series.

What is the latency? +

Over USB, PenCast achieves approximately 5ms latency. Over WiFi on a good local network, typically under 20ms — comparable to professional wired drawing tablets.

Can teachers use PenCast for presentations? +

Yes. The transparent overlay mode lets you draw and annotate directly on top of any open window on your screen. Laser pointer and highlighter are included for free — no Pro needed.

Is PenCast better than a Wacom tablet? +

PenCast turns a device you already own into a drawing tablet — saving you $80–$200 on a Wacom. Full pressure, tilt, hover, and palm rejection at a fraction of the cost.

How does the connection work? +

Three modes: WiFi with auto-discovery via QR code, USB via ADB for ultra-low latency, and Bluetooth BLE (Pro) for wireless use without WiFi.
